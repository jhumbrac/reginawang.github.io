Artist

Ma, Weidu

Native of Shandong Rongcheng, Ma is an art collector, antique connoisseur, CCTV "Lecture Room" speaker for "Ma Weidu and Art Collection". Ma is a member of China Democratic National Construction Association, founder and curator at GuanFu Museum, columnist at "China Network", and author of several best-sellers.
