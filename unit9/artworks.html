Xuan ("mysterious")

  • Medium: Ru Porcelain
  • Listed: 11/11/2014

Description

Artwork by famous collector and curator, Mr. Ma Weidu. "Mysterious" from Tao Te Ching of Laozi: "unknown is the world's beginning; "Mysterious" represents the transformative process for things to develop from scratch, from which we can ultimately find a way to resolve different things.
